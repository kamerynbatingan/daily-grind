A JavaScript Example by Kameryn Batingan

This pages uses HTML/CSS & JavaScript to create a single page that rotates unique colors and content for each weekday (Sunday to Saturday) into the page. The content includes:

  • One unique image, with appropriate and matching content in the alt tag.
  • A paragraph of content that describes the daily item, including the day of the week.
  • A unique color that supports the image and paragraph of content
